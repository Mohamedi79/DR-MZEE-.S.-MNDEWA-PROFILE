About Me

Dr. Mndewa holds a Doctorate (Ph.D.) in Optoelectronic Information Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in Wuhan, China; a Master's Degree in Information Technology from Griffith University in Queensland, Australia; a Bachelor's Degree in Science in Electronics from Osmania University in Hyderabad, India; and a Full Technician Certificate in Electrical Engineering from Karume Technical College in Zanzibar.

He is currently the Director of Communications in the Ministry of Infrastructure, Communications and Transport in Zanzibar. He has been a member of the National ICT Broadband Backbone (NICTBB) technical expert team since 2004. Other positions he has held include: Director of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) at the University of Dodoma; ICT consultant to the Government of Tanzania; and a member and coordinator of the technical team for network and infrastructure for the e-Government project in Zanzibar.
